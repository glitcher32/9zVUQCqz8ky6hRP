Frequently Asked Questions

Why is my search bar colored on different websites?

This is a feature called color mode. It takes the favicon of the website and tries to guess a color that would match the website. Sometimes it's wrong. If you want to turn it off go to General Settings and change the "Color Mode" setting.

Where are the back and forward buttons?

To find the back and forward buttons, simply swipe right from the left side of the screen and you will see the buttons at the bottom, or simply just press the square button at the top that shows the amount of tabs open.

Why is Request 'Do Not Track' grayed out in settings?

Unfortunately, only Android version kitkat 4.4 and up support this feature. If you're on a lower version, it won't work because it breaks page loading.

Why is Remove Identifying Headers grayed out in settings?

Unfortunately, only Android version kitkat 4.4 and up support this feature. If you're on a lower version, it won't work because it breaks page loading.

Why is Block 3rd Party Cookies grayed out in settings?

This is a new feature to the WebView and is only supported on Android version Lollipop 5.0 and up.

Can I save passwords on G-Browser?

Not currently, no.

Does G-Browser track me?

Not at all. I believe that all users should have their rights to privacy, that is why there isn't a single built in tracker in G-Browser. Privacy is a freedom we all should have.

Is G-Browser the safest browser out there?

No, of course not. Browsers like "Privacy Browser", "Tor Browser", and "DuckDuckGo" are much more built to be for privacy. G-Browser is simply just a safe and fast way to browse the web.

Nope, one isn't needed because nothing is tracked.
