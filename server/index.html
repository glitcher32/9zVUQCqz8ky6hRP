About us

G-Factions is a server that lets players create their own base, town, or even country and survive. You can also join other Player's faction group and be on their team. Coins can be earned by selling or even simply playing the game, and can be spent in the in game shop. If you need extra coins, you can complete challenges, or parkour for a prize.
