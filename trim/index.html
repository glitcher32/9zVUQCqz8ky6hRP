Support the processing of the following video formats - MP4,WEBM,MOV,FLV,3GP,M4V,MPG,MPEG,MKV,AVI,WMV,MV,DV,ASF,3G2
There are four steps to use this tool to process video files:
  1. The first step is to open the video file. Please check the supported file formats.
  2. In the second step, select the function, you can cut, convert, rotate and so on. Selecting different functions will bring up different operation interfaces.
  3. The third step is to set the parameters and click the button to execute.
  4. The fourth step, wait for the processing to complete, click the button to save the video to your computer.
 Browser-based Video Cutter
Free, online, and free of installation. There is no need to download software, no Flash plug-ins, or frequent software updates. It can be used by opening the browser.
 Data Privacy and Security
Using HTML5 and FFMPEG.JS technology, the content of the video file is read directly on the browser and processed directly on the browser Does not upload video data to any server. Your video files do not leave your device.
 Simple and Powerful
Support most video formats, can handle most of the current mainstream video files. Support mainstream audio and video codecs.
